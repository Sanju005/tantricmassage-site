What Is Lingam Massage?

Lingam massage originates from traditional tantric wellness practices that focus on relaxation, mindfulness, energy awareness, and stress reduction. The term "Lingam" is a Sanskrit word associated with masculine energy and is commonly referenced within traditional tantric philosophies.

A professional wellness-focused lingam massage session emphasizes relaxation, breathing, mindfulness, body awareness, and emotional well-being. Many clients choose this type of therapy as part of their self-care and wellness routine.
